Our Mission

To enhance the community and build community pride and unity as well as to empower the people by providing jobs and job training. The ultimate goal is to provide both spiritual and practical development and growth.

Our Vision

El Palais Bu-Sché was a vision created by Pastor Willie J. Sams after the passing of his wife the late Elnora Sams. Pastor Sams’ vision was to give the people of the community space worthy of their stature.
