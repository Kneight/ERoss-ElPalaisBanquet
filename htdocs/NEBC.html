Neighborhood Empowerments for Better Communities


Our Mission

To change, prepare, encourage, and motivate individuals and families for today’s workforce and society through programs and services.

Our Vision

To develop the economic well-being and quality of life for Youth 14 and up; to socially and economically develop youth and support adults for today’s society; to help the target group achieve as much as they can, as well as they can, as long as they can.
